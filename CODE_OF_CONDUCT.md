# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.
Contributors must communicate constructively, respect privacy and intellectual
property, accept technical disagreement in good faith, and avoid discriminatory,
threatening, or disruptive behavior.

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and community interactions
that violate these standards. Serious or sensitive incidents should be reported
privately through the repository host. Maintainers will review reports promptly,
protect reporter privacy where possible, and apply proportionate consequences.
